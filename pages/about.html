US Air Force

I served in the Air Force from 2011 - 2015 as a Geospatial Intelligence Analyst. The Geospatial Intelligence Analyst has many responsibilities, but a brief overview is that the analysts receive images taken by various methods, then analyze and exploit the images to generate critical, time-sensitive intelligence reports, and sending them to relevant parties or agencies. Geospatial Analysts may also be responsible for observing and monitoring the live-feed from other aircraft as well, and participating in reconnaissance missions. Serving in the military was one of the most worthwhile endeavors I have undertaken in my life so far, and I am proud to be able to call myself a veteran.
